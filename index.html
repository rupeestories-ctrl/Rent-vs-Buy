How this is calculated

Amortization. Month-level principal and interest. PMI applies if down payment is below 20% and cancels once balance falls below 78% of original value. Optional refinance rebuilds amortization at the new rate and term.

Taxes. Uses 2026 standard deduction defaults of $32,200 MFJ and $16,100 Single. SALT cap is editable. Federal capital gains bracket is based on taxable income at sale, with NIIT and state capital gains added if selected.

Rental conversion. Cash flow deducts principal and interest. Taxable income deducts interest and depreciation, not principal. Depreciation uses building value over 27.5 years. Sale after rental conversion includes depreciation recapture and §121 eligibility based on the 2-out-of-5-year rule.

Exit modeling. H1B and GC-pending scenarios apply exit friction and flat exit costs. Exit strategy can be sell, rent out, or hold vacant. In hold-vacant mode, the mortgage continues amortizing after the exit year using the active mortgage rate at exit, including any refinance that already occurred.

Not financial advice. This is a planning model. Tax outcomes can vary by state, income, passive loss rules, AMT, FIRPTA details, and whether you keep the home as a rental.
