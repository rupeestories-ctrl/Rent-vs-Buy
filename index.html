    How the Math Works

    A plain-English check so the result does not feel like a black box.
    Buying net worth = Home value - mortgage balance - selling costs - capital gains tax Renting net worth = Down payment invested + buying closing costs invested (includes buyer move cost) + security deposit - renter moving cost + selected share of annual cash-flow difference invested - estimated investment tax at exit (tax applies only to gains above total invested contributions) Annual buyer cash outflow = Principal + interest mortgage payment + property tax + PMI + maintenance + fixed repairs + insurance + HOA + extra owner utilities - actual tax benefit Annual renter cash outflow = Rent (with optional reset after plan year) + renter insurance Annual renter savings = Annual buyer cash outflow - annual renter cash outflow Invested renter savings = Annual renter savings x investment behavior percentage
